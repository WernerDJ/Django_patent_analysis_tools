I am a patent professional with over 14 years of experience specializing in the life sciences sector.

My journey in intellectual property began in the biotech investment space, where I spent a decade deepening my expertise in patent strategy

and analysis. Since then, I’ve continued to collaborate with a range of innovative companies, including a personalized medicine startup,

that could deliver exactly that. Over the years, I’ve developed data visualizations and analysis methods that go far beyond what standard

IP reports offer.
